Cources We Offer

Management and Computer Science Department

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration is a three years undergraduate program designed to offer complete knowledge about business and principles of management to students. It is a most valued degree especially for the students who are looking for a leadership position. The course provides the basic for Management in all the core areas of business.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Application is a three years undergraduate program designed to prepare student for a professional career in Computing and Information Technology. It gives an indepth knowledge of software engineering. Information system and the practical skills to apply the knowledge. In this Programm students are exposed to various areas of Computer Applications.

BSC(ECS)

Bachelor of Entire Computer Science is a three years undergraduate program designed to train future professionals, rather than more 'degree holders'.
This cource fulfill the needs of e-commerce in industries where the student would ultimately find employment and therefore has a completely different approach to learning.
