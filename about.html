About Institute


Shri. Sangameshwar Education Society's Sangameshwar College, affiliated to Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University, Solapur was established in the year June 1953 by two Karmayogies: Late Shri. Nagappa B Kadadi and Late Shri. Madeppa B Kadadi with a vision, 'To Spread education in general and instruct in any branch of knowledge whenever and wherever feasible'.




The institute is standing splendid and serene at the Historical Saat Rasta Chowk of Solapur, bearing around six decades of testimony to higher education, functioning with the mission, 'To provide suitable environment where students can develop their creativity, intellectuality, capability to tackle the problems with acuteness of sensitivity and to develop all-round personality, so that they can react to all the sensual phenomenon in a way that will make their hidden capabilities blossom forth'.

About Department

Management and Computer Science

With the vision of imparting quality education in management studies, the Management Department initiated the BBA Program in the academic year 2006 - 07. BBA Program is a three-year full time Bachelor Degree in Business Administration. The program is designed to bridge the gap between conceptual learning and practical application to inculcate professional and managerial expertise. The curriculum has been suitably modified to meet the challenging needs of the Indian Business Society. The curriculum reflects the influence of political, social, legal, technological and natural environmental issues on business world. It strengthens students in oral and written communication skills, accounting skills, analytical skills, statistical skills, computer skills, and focus on developing interpersonal, leadership, team building and other life skills.

-Mr. G.D.Jugdar,
convener of Managment Department

The Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) program was introduced in the year 2001 with the view of growing demand of IT professionals in the area of computer application.

The Bachelor of Entire Computer Science (BSc (ECS)) program was started in the academic year 2001 and MSc (CS) was started in the academic year 2006 with the aim to create quality professionals and research fellows who can work in every sector of the world by implementing the technology of computer system software. This degree can lead you to profiles like computer scientist, an information system manager, networking specialist who draws the technological roadmap for the organisation, ensures efficient management of the available computer facilities, handle smooth functioning of the local area and wide area networking, implement cybersecurity systems, look after system upgrading, system designing and technical analysis for the organisation. All stakeholders including the Faculty (Teaching and Non-Teaching), alumni and parents have a major role in the development of department.
